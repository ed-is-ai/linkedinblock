## Notes

- No requirements were dropped or adjusted during this milestone
- The hook-story pattern uses `I was \w+ing` (progressive form) rather than `I was in a meeting` to avoid false positives on meeting references — a subtle implementation detail documented in 17-04-SUMMARY.md
- Scoring caps (20+15+12=47 max from voice alone) allow a post to reach threshold only when two or more voice signals fire together, reducing false positives
