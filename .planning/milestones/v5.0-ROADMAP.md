# Milestone v5.0: Voice Pattern Detection

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-31
**Phases:** 17
**Total Plans:** 4

## Overview

Extends the heuristic detector with three new signal functions that catch AI-generated posts written in the distinctive "LinkedIn voice" — hook-story openers, motivational punch-rhythm paragraphs, and impersonal third-person authority framing. These posts previously scored 0 because they contain none of the listicle/buzzword/em-dash signals from v1.0. The integration test confirms a representative AI voice post scores 61 (threshold ≥ 60).

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Signal scoring caps: hook-story up to 20 pts, motivational up to 15 pts, impersonal up to 12 pts
- Regex patterns for hook-story require `I was \w+ing` form (not "I was in a meeting") — prevents false positives on meeting references
- Signal functions placed in `src/content/detector/signals/` alongside existing em-dash, buzzword, etc.

**Issues Resolved:**
